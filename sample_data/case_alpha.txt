Acme Components LLC v. Titan Logistics Inc.
United States District Court for the District of Colorado
Case No. 26-cv-1184

COMPLAINT FOR BREACH OF CONTRACT AND DECLARATORY RELIEF

Plaintiff Acme Components LLC alleges defendant Titan Logistics Inc. failed to perform under a supply agreement dated January 10, 2024.
Plaintiff seeks damages in excess of $4,500,000 plus consequential damages and attorneys' fees.
The agreement provides that the prevailing party in any action may recover reasonable attorneys' fees and costs.

Plaintiff alleges a commercial dispute between two businesses regarding fulfillment, pricing, and inventory commitments.
